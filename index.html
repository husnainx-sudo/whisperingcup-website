Where Every Sip
Tells a Story

Just a whisper away from UMT, The Whispering Cup is where the best coffee meets great conversation. We believe that the best moments happen over a carefully crafted cup; whether it's a steaming latte on a rainy evening or an iced coffee on a scorching Lahore afternoon.

From our handpicked beans to our cozy interiors, every detail is designed to make you feel at home. Come for the coffee, stay for the stories.
